# Patterns Board

> A running log of signals from Discussions, Issues, and PRs. Used to evolve what's in `samples/` and the canonical doc over time — driven by usage, not by any one maintainer's taste.
>
> **Nothing auto-promotes or auto-relegates.** Entries below are candidates; humans decide in the **Decisions** table at the bottom.

## Thresholds

- **Promotion candidate** — a pattern (role, skill, hook, routine, MCP wiring) shows up in **3+ independent Discussions / Issues / PRs**, or garners clear enthusiasm (reactions, forks, comments).
- **Relegation candidate** — a pattern already in `samples/` that hasn't been referenced, forked, or extended in **6+ months**.
- **Refinement candidate** — a pattern appearing repeatedly but in varying forms — worth consolidating into a single canonical version.

## How to flag a candidate

- **Notice a pattern recurring?** Comment on the relevant Discussion or Issue referencing this board, or open a new Issue labelled `patterns-board`.
- **Maintainers review the board periodically** (monthly target, ad hoc in practice) and move items into the Decisions table.
- **Promoted patterns** get a PR adding them to `samples/` or an `ADOPTION.md` section.
- **Relegated patterns** get a PR removing or archiving them, with a link to the recorded Decision.
